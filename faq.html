Frequently Asked Questions

Is Snuggly suitable for my senior citizen?

There’s no need for your pet to act their age when they dine on Snuggly wholefoods! For a senior citizen, it’s essential to maintain your pet’s body weight to help prevent diseases. We customise serving sizes to to ensure they continue maintaining their ideal weight. Snuggly’s bowls nourish seniors with natural nutrients to help them thrive in the older years. For example, Snuggly has a high omega 3 – omega 6 ratio, which helps lower chronic inflammation and reduce risk of many later-onset diseases such as cancer and arthritis.

What does it mean that Snuggly is complete and balanced?

Complete and balanced means that Snuggly exceeds all of the nutritional requirements your pet’s needs, from macronutrients (protein, fat and carbohydrates) to micronutrients (vitamins and minerals). Our recipes follow the AAFCO nutritional standards and are designed to grow with your pet from their baby years, all the way to their golden oldies. Our recipes are also crossed checked with the FEDIAF (European Pet Food Industry Federation) standards. We work with leading holistic vets, as well as an Australian veterinary nutritionist, combining the best of natural medicine and scientific research to formulate our recipes. We craft our recipes to help optimise your pet’s health for a longer, happier life. Snuggly’s recipes undergo testing through nutritional software and food laboratories to guarantee their nutritional content.

Where do you source Snuggly’s ingredients?

Feel-good food starts with fresh ingredients. We’re proud to support Australian businesses and 85-95% of each recipe contains Australian-grown ingredients. Our meat and vegetables are sourced locally from Australian farmers and our dry ingredients such as seeds and superfoods are also sourced locally where possible.

Is Snuggly raw?

No, Snuggly is a fresh, lightly cooked diet. Our meats and vegetables are prepared the same way we would cook our own food. Our seeds, oils, herbs, and other ingredients are added raw. Raw meat poses food safety risks, such as potential contamination through pathogens and bacteria. Some raw food companies may not produce balanced food, which may lead to pet getting long-term deficiencies in certain nutrients. Plus, some pets are particularly sensitive to a raw diet and are not able to handle consuming raw meat. Snuggly bowls are the best of all three worlds: complete and balanced meals made from clean ingredients, without the risk of bacteria and pathogens, and gentle on sensitive tummies.

When will I be charged for my purchase?

If you are an ongoing subscriber, the Sunday midnight cut-off before your next box remains the same.

Is your packaging sustainable?

We take sustainability and freshness very seriously here at Snuggly HQ, so we’re very proud to say that our pouches are 100% biodegradable. Throw them in your regular bin and they’ll break down in 30 weeks from the time they were produced – much faster than the 2 years it takes a banana peel!
